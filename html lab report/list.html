How to Prepare a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Place a tea bag in a cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water into the cup over the tea bag.
  4. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Remove the tea bag and discard it.
  6. Add sugar, milk, or lemon to taste, if desired.
  7. Stir and enjoy your tea.
